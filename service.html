Our Services

OMS & AS Pvt Ltd, commonly known as “OMS” has sufficient teams & most experiences persons offering expertise in a wide range of cargo supervision survey, Container inspections, marine surveyors, loss assessors and claim settling & others allied services like man power supply services to factory premises as security, helpers, engineering work & electrician etc.

IMPORT CONSIGNMENT:

  1. Draught Survey / Bunker Survey of Off-Hire, On-Hire, Conditions, Supply Survey.

    We perform accurate draught and bunker surveys for on-hire / off-hire, supply checks and condition reporting with daily records and certified reports.

    Detailed measurement, tank checks and certified documentation.
  2. Discharging Supervision either bulk cargo, projects, steels and containers

    We provide end-to-end discharging supervision ensuring safe, accurate and efficient unloading of bulk cargo, steel materials, project cargo and containers, while maintaining real-time records and operational control.

    Continuous oversight, cargo condition verification and well-documented discharge records.
  3. Godown-wise or stowage wise supervision

    We monitor godown-wise and stowage-wise operations to ensure proper cargo placement, secure handling, accurate documentation, and smooth movement inside storage areas. Our team supervises cargo allocation across different godowns and stowage locations, ensuring correct stacking, damage prevention, space optimization, and compliance with client instructions.

    Efficient cargo placement, accurate records and safe stowage handling.
  4. Bagging & Standardization supervision specially for fertilizers & Agro products

    We supervise bagging and standardization processes to ensure accurate weight, proper sealing, uniform packing quality and contamination-free handling, especially for fertilizers and agro products. Our team oversees the complete bagging operation, monitoring weight accuracy, packaging quality, product standardization and correct labeling to maintain consistency and meet client specifications for fertilizers and agro commodities.

    Accurate weighing, uniform packing and contamination-free handling.
  5. Loading supervision either by road or rake

    We supervise loading operations by road and rake to ensure correct vehicle placement, accurate weighment, safe loading practices, proper documentation, and continuous monitoring until dispatch is completed. Our team monitors loading activities for both road transportation and railway rakes, checking vehicle conditions, verifying material weight, ensuring proper stacking, preventing losses and delays, and maintaining complete loading records for clients.

    Safe loading, accurate weighment and clear dispatch records.
  6. Destination supervision across India

    We provide destination supervision across India, ensuring proper cargo receiving, weight verification, condition checking and timely reporting at the final delivery point as per client instructions.

    Our destination supervision team monitors cargo arrival at various locations across India, verifies quantity and condition, checks documentation, ensures safe unloading and delivers accurate final reports to assist in transparent and smooth delivery operations.

    Safe unloading, quantity validation and transparent final documentation.
  7. Coal Supervision Survey & Analysis as per client requirement

    We provide complete coal supervision and analysis services, including sampling, moisture testing, quality verification and quantity assessment, ensuring accurate reporting as per client requirements and industry standards.

    Our coal supervision team conducts systematic sampling, laboratory analysis, moisture determination, size distribution checks and loading/unloading monitoring to ensure precise quality control and reliable results tailored to client specifications.

    Reliable coal analysis with monitored handling and certified reporting.
